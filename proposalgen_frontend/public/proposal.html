Section 02 — What to Expect

The Four Phases
of Your Project

Phase 01
Research & Outreach
We gather deep intelligence about your company, your customers, and your market. Focus groups, audience research, and brand analysis form the bedrock of everything that follows.
Phase 02
Framework & Design
Sitemaps, wireframes, UX flows, and UI design. [Client.Company] plays an active role here — it's your vision we're realising, and getting this right saves significant time later.
Phase 03
Development
Our engineers get to work. This phase is intensive and largely internal, but every line of code is written against the agreed specification. [Client.Company] re-engages at testing.
Phase 04
Testing & Launch
Rigorous QA, bug resolution, and a collaborative marketing strategy ensure your app launches to an audience ready and waiting. We go live together.

Framework Highlights

  • Functionality mapping and content architecture
  • Interactive wireframes — the structural core of your app
  • Branding integration with existing digital platforms (web, email, social)
  • User Experience (UX) and User Interface (UI) design — how it feels to use, and why people love it
