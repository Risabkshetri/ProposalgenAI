Notes from the call:

Rahul wants us to build a local grocery delivery app system for DailyFresh. The main goal is to let their local customers order groceries online and get them delivered the same day by DailyFresh's own delivery staff.



Basically, there are three main parts. 

First, the Customer App. People should be able to sign up with OTP, browse categories, see product details, and add to cart. Checkout is strictly Cash on Delivery (COD) only. They need to enter full addresses (house no, street, landmark) and track their order status (Placed, Confirmed, Packed, Out for Delivery, Delivered). Also need basic push notifications.



Second, the Delivery Boy App. The riders need to log in, see which orders are assigned to them, view customer details, and call them directly from the app. They just need buttons to update status to "Picked" or "Delivered" and a way to confirm they collected the COD cash. No GPS live tracking needed, just address-based delivery.



Third, the Admin/Staff Panel. Rahul needs a web dashboard to manage products, inventory, accept orders, and manually assign orders to specific delivery boys. He also wants to manage delivery boy accounts, see customer lists, and get basic sales reports. Oh, we also need a small login for the warehouse staff just so they can see incoming orders and mark them as "Packed".



Delivery model is same-day, internal riders, manual assignment.



Tech stack we agreed on:

Mobile Apps in Flutter (both customer and rider)

Admin panel in React.js

Backend in Node.js with MongoDB

We will host it on AWS for them.
